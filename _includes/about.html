What is Of10?

Of10 is a design-led, contemporary lifestyle brand and the industry innovator in the field of collaborative workspaces.

We are a curated co-working space located in Hiranandani Gardens, Powai, Mumbai. We provide the ultimate in insider access, allowing innovators and local tastemakers to be connected to the pulse of What’s New and Next.

Each Of10 space offers a unique mix of innovative design centred around design, fashion, music and tech.
